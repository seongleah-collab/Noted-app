How it works

Before, during, and after every conversation.

Before

Prep mode

Pull in web research on the people you are meeting, generate questions to ask, and set the note style you want before anything begins.

During

Live mode

Watch notes appear in real time, flag deadlines and assignments, and ask follow-up questions while the lecture or meeting is still in motion.

After

Output mode

Get polished summaries, to-dos, reminders, emails to send, and a permanent page that explains exactly what happened.

Built for both sides

Made for lectures and meetings, without forcing one workflow onto the other.

For lectures

  • Catch assignments, deadlines, and exam details as they happen
  • Analyze recorded lectures later and merge them with your older notes
  • Choose how detailed your class notes should be
  • Stay present instead of scrambling to capture every line

For meetings

  • Walk in with instant background research on who you are meeting
  • See action items, decisions, and follow-ups in real time
  • Ask the assistant about the conversation while it is happening
  • Leave with draft emails, reminders, and organized next steps
