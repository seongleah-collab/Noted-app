Features

Everything you need.
Nothing anyone can see.

Noted captures audio, reads your screen, takes notes, answers questions, and tracks action items — all in real time, all completely invisible.

Real-time notes, your way

Notes generate live as your meeting or lecture happens — not after the fact. Choose your depth, style, and format. Request changes mid-session so you're never stuck with notes you don't want.

Context-aware chatbot

Ask questions mid-meeting and get answers grounded in what's actually being said. "What did she just say about the exam?" — answered instantly.

Completely incognito

No bots join your call. No one gets notified. No screen indicators. Noted captures system audio locally — invisible to everyone but you.

Action items & reminders

Automatically detects deadlines, to-dos, and assignments. Everything gets organized with dates so nothing slips through.

Screen context + audio

Sees your screen and hears audio simultaneously. Notes reference what's shown — "the chart John presented on slide 3" — 10x richer than audio-only.

Learns how you think

Over time, Noted adapts to your vocabulary, your note-taking preferences, and how you like things explained. It builds off the way you think — creating a truly personal AI assistant that no competitor offers.

Two modes, one tool

Meeting mode.
Lecture mode.

The way notes are structured and what the AI prioritizes changes depending on the mode you choose.

Meeting Mode

Conversational notes built for back-and-forth discussions. Tracks who said what, surfaces action items, and generates follow-up emails. Before your meeting, Noted pulls background info on attendees so you walk in prepared.

  • Conversation-style structured notes
  • Pre-meeting attendee research
  • Auto-generated follow-up emails
  • Suggested questions to ask

Lecture Mode

Study-ready notes with definitions, formulas, key concepts, and structured outlines. Perfect for classes, webinars, and training sessions where you need to absorb information.

  • Definitions, formulas & key terms
  • Structured study outlines
  • Assignment tracking with due dates
  • Cross-session memory across lectures

Privacy first

Your data stays yours.
Period.

Noted is built from the ground up to keep your information private and under your control.

On-device processing

Audio transcription and AI note generation happen entirely on your machine. Nothing gets sent to a server. This means Noted works offline too — record a lecture on a plane and still get full notes.

Zero-knowledge design

We can't see your notes, recordings, or transcripts. Everything is encrypted and stored locally on your device.

User-owned data

Export everything — notes, transcripts, calendars — to Notion, Google Drive, or wherever you want. Your data is never locked in.
